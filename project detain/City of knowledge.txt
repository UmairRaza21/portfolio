### Course Coordinator – City of Knowledge Institute

At City of Knowledge Institute, I managed and coordinated professional training courses, ensuring smooth operations from student enrollment to course completion. I worked closely with students and instructors to provide an organized learning experience while maintaining accurate records and schedules.

My responsibilities included handling course planning, student support, communication, and day-to-day administrative tasks. I focused on solving operational challenges, improving coordination, and ensuring courses were delivered efficiently and on time.

**Key Contributions**

* Managed end-to-end course operations and coordination.
* Assisted students throughout the enrollment and learning process.
* Scheduled classes and coordinated with instructors.
* Maintained student records and course documentation.
* Resolved administrative and communication challenges efficiently.
* Contributed to a smooth and professional learning environment.
